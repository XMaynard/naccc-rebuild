Crude oil compliance:

It's about the documentation

Under the North American Free Trade Agreement, Canadian crude oil can enter the United States duty free with the appropriate documentation. Crude oil companies have had a challenge in acquiring appropriate documentation to support their trade preference claims. As a result, a significant amount of crude oil which should be entered duty free, enter the United States duty paid because companies do not have the appropriate documentation to support a claim of trade preference under the NAFTA.

NACCC addresses this issue by providing crude oil companies with documentation to support their claims of trade preference. Contact us to learn more.

What do we do?

North American Crude Compliance Corp. (NACCC) is an Alberta company engaged in the documentation of crude oil and condensate for trade compliance purposes under the NAFTA.

NACCC provides trade compliance services to crude oil companies. Our services include:

  • Statement of origin documentation for condensate and crude oil
  • Development and monthly management of inventory management systems
  • Conducting pre-verification reviews for companies wishing to ensure that they have the appropriate documentation to support their NAFTA preference claims.

NACCC services address the significant compliance issue facing the crude oil industry.

We have acquired the appropriate documentation to support trade preference claims under the NAFTA. Contact us to learn more.
